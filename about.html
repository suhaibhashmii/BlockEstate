What We Build

Trustless Real Estate on Ethereum

BlockEstate is a decentralised application that brings real estate transactions on-chain. Using Ethereum smart contracts, every property registration, listing, and acquisition is recorded immutably — no intermediaries, no hidden fees, no delays.

The platform integrates with MetaMask for seamless wallet authentication and leverages Ganache for local blockchain development and testing, providing a complete end-to-end experience for secure property ownership transfer.

Technology

Ethereum · Solidity · Ethers.js · MetaMask · Ganache

Security

All transactions validated on-chain. Ownership immutably recorded.
